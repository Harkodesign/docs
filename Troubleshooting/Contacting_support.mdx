## How to Reach Out

- **Direct Contact:** Use the 'Contact Us' feature in the app or on the website for direct communication with our support team.
- **Email Support:** Send your queries or issues to our dedicated support email address, available in the app and on the website.

## Best Practices

- **Detail Your Issue:** Provide a clear and concise description of your problem, including any relevant screenshots or error messages.
- **Patience and Follow-Up:** Our team strives to respond promptly. If you haven't received a response within a reasonable timeframe, feel free to send a follow-up inquiry.

Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you with any issues or questions you may have while using 'Imagine'.
