## Purpose of the Guidelines

To cultivate a respectful, inclusive, and supportive environment where creativity thrives and all members feel valued and inspired.

## Respect and Courtesy

- **Principle:** Maintain a high standard of respect and kindness in all interactions.
- **Prohibited Behavior:** Harassment, bullying, or any discrimination based on race, gender, sexuality, or other personal characteristics are strictly prohibited.
- **Expectation:** Contribute to a positive and supportive atmosphere, valuing diverse opinions and backgrounds.

## Constructive Feedback

- **Encouragement:** Offer feedback that is constructive, aimed at helping others improve their work.
- **Prohibited Behavior:** Refrain from negative remarks that could harm the community's spirit of learning and improvement.
- **Objective:** Create a nurturing space for growth, where feedback is a tool for development, not discouragement.

## Intellectual Property

- **Expectation:** Uphold the highest standards of intellectual property respect.
- **Prohibited Behavior:** Using copyrighted materials without permission is not allowed.
- **Reminder:** Always credit original creators and seek appropriate licenses for any third-party content.

## Privacy and Confidentiality

- **Expectation:** Respect the privacy and confidentiality of all community members.
- **Prohibited Behavior:** Sharing personal information of others without consent is strictly forbidden.
- **Guideline:** Be mindful of the information you share and how it affects others' privacy.

## No Spam or Self-Promotion

- **Guidelines:** Focus on contributing positively to the community rather than spamming or excessive self-promotion.
- **Encouragement:** Engage in meaningful discussions, share ideas, and contribute to the collective knowledge of the community.

## Reporting and Enforcement

- **Process:** Clear instructions for reporting inappropriate behavior or content.
- **Response:** 'Imagine' will take appropriate actions against guideline violations, including warnings, content removal, or account suspension.

Adhering to these guidelines is essential for maintaining a positive, productive, and enriching environment for all members of the 'Imagine' community.
